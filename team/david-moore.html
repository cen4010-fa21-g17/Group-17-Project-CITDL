Meet David

Hello! I'm a post-baccalaureate computer science major at Florida Atlantic University. I began my degree program in the Fall of 2019. I'm 39-years old. My degree program will allow me to change careers after teaching for fourteen years in public education with Broward County Public Schools in South Florida and ten years of military service in the US Army. I completed four tours of duty overseas. I retired from the Army in May 2021 as a Logistics SGT (Sergeant) E-5. On the fun side of things, I love science fiction films, reading, biking, and spending time with my dog Star. He is a beautiful Coton de Tulear.
It has been rewarding to get to learn about each member of CITDL and collaborate. I believe we will be successful together.

Skill Set

I have beginner level training and experience in Web Development (frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript; backend: MySQL, relational database design and management, Object Orientation, Ruby framework, Rack, and PHP), Git, and academic coding assignment experience the following languages: Python, C++, C, and Java.
In terms of soft skills, I can highlight three of my strongest: effective communication, conflict resolution, team building. I am an introvert and strive to play to my strengths as such. As far as weaknesses I want to overcome, I resist leading people. I prefer to work alone to a fault at times. I have yet to find a way to adapt to quick change without falling prey to "analysis paralysis." But overall, I am proud of my honest awareness and determination to grow personally, academically, and in the professionally in the workplace.
