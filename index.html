About The Team

CITDL (pronounced like "citadel") is a team of undergraduate engineering majors enrolled in Principles of Software Engineering (CEN4010) at Florida Atlantic University's College of Engineering and Computer Science.
This about page is for the purpose of introducing ourselves, and to fulfill our first milestone as a group.

Our Mission

As a team we are tasked with a semester-long group exercise to put into practice the skills, tools, and ethics required of an engineering team in a real-world corporate setting.
Over the course of the semester we will learn then execute a series of milestones that will culminate with an actual software engineering product. Our final product will be presented at the end of the semester.
